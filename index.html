Panda Express 2025 Menu: New Dishes, Deals, and More!

Flavorful Combo Bowls and Entrees for Every Taste

Panda Express has perfected the art of mixing bold flavors in their combo bowls. A fan favorite is the Orange Chicken, with its crispy exterior and tangy sweetness, paired perfectly with sides like Chow Mein or Fried Rice. For those who enjoy a lighter meal, the Super Greens are an excellent choice, offering a fresh and healthy alternative to traditional sides.

Tasty Sides to Complement Your Meal

No Panda Express meal is complete without a side dish. The popular Chow Mein and Fried Rice are staples, but Steamed White Rice and Super Greens are great healthier alternatives. These sides provide the right mix of flavor, texture, and nutrition, making them perfect additions to any meal.

Family Meals: Feeding the Whole Family with Panda Express

The Family Meal option includes three hearty entrees and two sides, designed to serve four to six people. It allows customization so that everyone can enjoy their favorite dishes. With generous portions and a variety of flavors, it’s the ideal choice for families or groups looking for a delicious and affordable meal.

Panda Express Kids’ Menu: Perfect Portions for Younger Diners

The Kids’ Menu offers smaller, kid-friendly portions of popular entrees like Orange Chicken, Kung Pao Chicken, and Grilled Teriyaki Chicken. Each meal includes a side and a drink, ensuring a balanced and nutritious option for young diners.
